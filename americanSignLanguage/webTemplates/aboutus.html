About HyperTalk

HyperTalk uses a machine learning algorithm code to define and trains a convolutional neural network model for ASL detection using Keras, faster than existing tools, making it an innovative and unique solution. The website's and mobile app's architecture is designed to be user-friendly, with a simple and intuitive interface that is easy to use and navigate.

The website and app will support real-time translation, allowing users to communicate with hearing individuals in the real-time and bidirectional ways with simulations for the patient.

Our team brings together experts in technology, design, and accessibility to create innovative solutions that make a real difference in people's lives. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible and to continuously improving our products and services.
