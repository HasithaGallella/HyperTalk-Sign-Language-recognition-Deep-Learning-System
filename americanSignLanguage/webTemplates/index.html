HOW IT ALL STARTED

Our journey began with a shared vision to bridge the gap between people and improve understanding through technology. Three students, each with a unique perspective, came together to create Hypertalk.

Although none of us had prior programming experience, we overcame this challenge through determination and support from our teachers and parents. Along the way, we relied on resources such as Google and YouTube to learn and grow.

Now, our project stands as a testament to the power of technology to connect and empower people. We are proud to have brought our idea to life, and we continue to work towards making the world a more inclusive place through our efforts with Hypertalk.



WHY HyperTalk?

The ultimate sign language communication app for individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing. Like a hero emerging with weapons, we are equipping people with the right translation tools to help them communicate in an increasingly accessible society.

Our cutting-edge mobile app uses advanced computer vision and image processing technology to facilitate seamless communication in sign language. With HyperTalk, individuals with hearing and talking issues can easily connect with others, breaking down the barriers of communication that have been holding them back. Whether you're at home, work or out in the world, HyperTalk makes it easy to engage with others and express yourself in sign language. It's time to emerge into a world of communication and connection with HyperTalk!
